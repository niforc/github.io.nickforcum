About

I've always been a creative & crafty being...I owe that to my lovely Grandma Wilma. I love getting my hands dirty and taking nothing and making it something special! I'm the girl that searched the woods for the ugly Christmas tree just so I could make it feel pretty. I've just recently quit my job as an accountant to craft full time... I wish I would have done this Long ago! I love waking up each day and remembering all over again that I am blessed to be doing what I love every day! Thank you for taking your time to check out my creations! Check us out on Facebook at Flawed to Fabulous and give us a like! More great items can be seen there.
